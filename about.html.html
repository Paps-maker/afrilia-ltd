About Us

We are the best exporter's of Agricultural products in Uganda

Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd is a leading agricultural company specializing in the cultivation, harvesting, and export of high-quality agricultural crops. With a commitment to sustainable farming practices, Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd. leverages advanced agricultural technologies and eco-friendly methods to produce a wide range of crops, including grains, fruits, vegetables, and nuts.The company operates extensive farms equipped with state-of-the-art irrigation systems and employs skilled agronomists to ensure optimal crop yields. Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd has established a robust supply chain that includes partnerships with local farmers, efficient logistics, and stringent quality control measures to maintain the highest standards.Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd. exports its produce to markets worldwide, meeting the diverse needs of its international clientele. The company prides itself on delivering fresh, nutritious, and pesticide-free crops, contributing to global food security and promoting sustainable agriculture. Through its dedicated efforts, Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd. aims to be a trusted partner in the global agricultural industry, fostering growth and innovation in the sector.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to deliver the finest Agricultural products to international markets by promoting sustainable farming practices, ensuring quality and safety at every stage of production, and fostering fair trade relationships. We are dedicated to innovation,customer satisfaction and contributing to the well-being of communities and the environment. We strive to enhance the global food system and provide our customers with products they can trust.

Our Vision

To be the leading exporter's in Uganda of high Quality Agricultural products, forstering sustainable farming practices and enriching lives by providing nutritious, safe and ethically-sourced produce to Comsumers worldwide.
