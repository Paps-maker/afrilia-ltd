Products

Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd exports a diverse range of high-quality agricultural crops, carefully cultivated to meet international standards. Their main exports include:
Grains: Wheat, corn, barley, and rice are grown using advanced farming techniques to ensure high yields and superior quality.
Fruits: Fresh and exotic fruits like apples, oranges, bananas, and mangoes, sourced from orchards that prioritize organic and sustainable practices.
Vegetables: A variety of vegetables, including tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, and bell peppers, known for their freshness and nutritional value.
Nuts: Almonds, walnuts, and peanuts, cultivated in optimal conditions to retain their rich flavor and health benefits.
Legumes: High-protein crops such as beans, lentils, and chickpeas, grown with precision to cater to the dietary needs of global markets.
Afrilia Agro Uganda Ltd ensures that all their exports are free from pesticides and comply with stringent quality control measures, guaranteeing that consumers receive only the best products.
